What even is an 'orrery'?
An orrery is a mechanical model that helps people to better understand the Solar System. It is generally scaled down, due to the huge distances between celestial bodies. The following picture presents what an orrery is.

Why are orreries important?
Using this method, you're able to compare the theoretical value of the orbits with the experimental one for detecting the presence of new, uncharted, celestial objects. With this method, it can also predict an asteroid dangerously approaching Earth, limiting the risks. And also, it's a fun way to learn about the planets of the solar system.
